Meeting Reports

In order for you to access the Meeting Report section, you must first select the business you wish to create the report for. Once you have done that, the Meeting Report Item will appear on the sidebar menu.

Work Reports

Once you have selected to view an existing Meeting Report, you will see a button on the top right corner. When you click it it will direct you to all the work reports that are related to the meeting report you selected previously.
